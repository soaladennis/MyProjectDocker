Anti-Pump & Dump

What follows most presale is a pump in price and massive dumping from whales and bots. Hypersonic token is loaded with features that strictly manages the behaviour and experience of investors.

The token has dynamic sell fees ranging from 70% to 0 fees thereby making it cost 70% fee to sell at the first day of getting the tokens. Check here for more info.

Anti-Whale

If every hodler at some point can never sell off their tokens, then Hypersonic would be on its way to colonise the moon.

The Anti-Sell Off feature makes it impossible to empty your wallet no matter how little the amount is. You will always be a hodler as you can only sell 95% of your balance at any point in time.

Anti-Pump & Dump

Supersonic Finance token is the pass code in the Supersonic ecosystem of products. Holding Hypersonic gives you more Supersonic ($SSN) tokens.

Tokens are rewarded every 6 hours. The contract sends from 8 - 37.1% of every transaction to the dividend pool for the rewards of active holders.

Automatic Liquidity Supply

A constant flow of liquidity back into the pool helps to keep the price impact healthy and provide support for active trading of the token.

On every transaction, the contract sends from 3% to 14% of the transaction amount to the liquidity pool in BNB - Hypersonic pair.

Minimum Amount for Reward

With no restrictions on who can receive rewards, we would have a lot of minute purchases which would be of no impact to the growth of the project.

This feature also helps to keep the reward to fewer number of holders. To get the rewards, you need to hold a minimum of 50 Hypersonic tokens - subject to review depending on token price in the future.

Anti-Whale

Automatic and auto transactional burning of tokens is underrated. If we really burn on every transaction, then we would be able to diminish the total supply significantly in a short period of time and hence increase the value of one token.

Hypersonic is the first token to implement this feature. For every transaction (buy / transfer), there is a 4% burn fee sent to the burn address. For every sell transaction, up to 18.9% of the transaction amount is sent to the burn address..
